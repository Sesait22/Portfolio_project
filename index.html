About Me

Hii! I'm Sesait, and I'am passionate about building things for the web. My journey started just a few months ago when I didn't have any idea on my career path. As the 20s can be a confusing age & I had just left school, I wanted to do something that made me have Fun while working & so after a lot of thinking I chose Web Development. I took an orientation course with DCI and finally made my decision to go with web development.
After the orientation course, I continued with the one year bootcamp course with DCI . And boyyyy was it just fun, it was exciting,tiring and many other emotions along side. However the emotions are what made me LOVE web development more & so I have chosen it forever. Even though my course is still ongoing,I feel like my learning speed has been & is quite fast and that I am able to accomplish things & this Portfolio is a witness to that.That is why I am now looking for a junior web dev position to finally kick start my career and learn among professionals.
